About



Share your fitness journey with ZenFoci; an optimized all-inclusive app with a focus on simplicity and clean design. ZenFoci allows you to keep track of your daily habits, workouts and offers you nutritious and customizable meal-plans from a range of well-known recipes.


ZenFoci allows you to track progress toward your nutrition, fitness, weight loss and water goals with simplicity. Behind all the time and careful precision that went into the app, ZenFoci aims to deliver a seamless food and fitness journal app where we hope is every minimalist's dream in both form and function!

Meals & Groceries


Our recipes are loaded with nutrients, fiber, and protein. They're designed to help you feel nourished and satiated. With just the click of a button, you will be able to navigate from our database and find yourself the perfect meal catered just for you (and even your loved ones!)
